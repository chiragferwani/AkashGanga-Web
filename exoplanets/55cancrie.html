55-Cancri-E

  • Distance: Approximately 41 light-years away from Earth.
  • Star: Orbits a star named 55 Cancri A in the constellation of Cancer.
  • Mass: Nearly 8 times the mass of Earth, making it a super-Earth.
  • Orbit Duration: Completes one orbit around its star in just 18 hours.
  • Temperature: Extremely hot surface, with temperatures reaching up to 2000°C (3600°F).
